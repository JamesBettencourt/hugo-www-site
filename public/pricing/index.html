Frequently Asked Questions:

You've got questions, we've got answers! If you need any help, you can chat with us.

Metricly charges per host server, and includes up to 150 metrics collected through Metricly data sources or agents. A host can be a physical or virtual (e.g. AWS EC2) server independent of the number of CPUs and cores. Metrics collected from the middleware, database, and application layers are included in the per-host fee.
We don’t limit user creation or the amount of users that you can have, so everyone on your team can have access to your account. We find this helps everyone get on the same page and avoids the trouble of having to share a login.
For standard accounts, Metricly keeps data on standard accounts for 12 months. For trial accounts, Metricly retains data for the duration of your trial.
Metricly’s product documentation walks through all setup and configuration details. We also offer our free Premier Services to all new customers to get them on-boarded. Should you need to submit a bug or speak live with a member of our support team, you can create a ticket in-product through the support pop up which is present on every page in the application.
Our policy is flexible in this area as compared to any other SaaS vendor. If your monthly usage is above your annual commitment volume, you are provided with a 7-day grace period to decide whether to increase your annual commitment, or pay for the hours of overage at the monthly price rate.
There are volume discounts available for enterprise accounts with over 100 hosts. Our sales team will be happy to work out the details with you.
When you pay for one host, the price includes the data that we collect from your cloud provider (example: EC2), the agent that you can install on your host to collect a rich set of metrics, and the plugins that you enable such as: RabbitMQ, NGINX, or Redis.
Each host can have up to 10 containers per hour at no additional cost. Additional containers can be purchased on a prepaid basis at the rate of $1/month for an annual contract, or hourly at $0.002 per hour.
